# Product Demo

Use for feature launches, product introductions, pitch decks, and technical
showcases where the audience needs to understand value and workflow.

## Slide Shapes

- Hero: product or feature name, concrete promise, product visual.
- User pain: before state, constraint, or workflow friction.
- Feature walkthrough: one capability per slide with screenshot or diagram.
- Before/after: paired panels with matching structure.
- Differentiators: three to four cards with icons or short labels.
- Proof: metric, customer quote summary, or benchmark chart.
- Call to action: next step, install command, or link.

## pptz Patterns

- Use images for real product states when available; use shapes only for
  abstract flows.
- Use rich text hyperlinks for docs, demos, or install links.
- Use icon/shape cards for differentiators, but keep card text short.
- Use charts sparingly and only for proof.

## Avoid

- Marketing copy that hides the product or workflow.
- Feature lists without a visual state or concrete user action.
- Screenshots too small to inspect.
